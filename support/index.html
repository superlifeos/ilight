Common Notes

  • Flashlight features depend on your device hardware and iOS permissions.
  • Screen Light uses your device display and may temporarily adjust screen brightness while active.
  • Compass accuracy depends on device sensors, magnetic interference, and location permission for true north.
  • SOS, strobe, and slow flash modes should be used responsibly. Bright or flashing light may be uncomfortable for some people.
