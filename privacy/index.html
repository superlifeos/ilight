iLight Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 7, 2026

iLight is designed as a simple flashlight and screen light utility. The app does not track you and does not collect personal data.

Data Collection

iLight does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data. The app does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Camera and Flashlight

iLight uses camera access only to control the device flashlight. The app does not take photos, record videos, save camera content, or upload camera content.

Location and Compass

iLight may request location permission to improve compass accuracy and show true north when available. Location information is used on the device for compass behavior and is not stored or shared by iLight.

Local Settings

Settings such as brightness, light mode, screen light choices, language, haptics, and display preferences are stored locally on your device. These settings are not sent to the developer or to third parties.

In-App Purchases

iLight may offer optional tip jar purchases through Apple In-App Purchase. Payments are processed by Apple. iLight does not receive or store your payment card details.

Support Email

If you contact support by email, your email address and any information you choose to include will be used only to respond to your request. You can request deletion of support emails by contacting hi.superlifeos@gmail.com.

Children

iLight is a general utility app and is not directed at children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Terms

Use of iLight is also subject to the Terms of Use.

Changes

This policy may be updated if iLight's features or data practices change. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact hi.superlifeos@gmail.com.
