Safety

Bright light and flashing light can be uncomfortable or unsafe for some people. Do not point the flashlight directly into eyes, especially around infants, children, pets, or anyone who may be sensitive to light.

Use SOS, strobe, and slow flash modes carefully. These modes may affect photosensitive users and should not be used to distract, harass, or endanger others.
